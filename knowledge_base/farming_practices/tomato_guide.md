# Tomato Cultivation Guide

## Pests and Diseases

### Tomato Leaf Curl Virus (ToLCV)
**Symptoms**: Leaves curl upwards, become small, and turn yellow. Plants become stunted.
**Vector**: Transmitted by Whitefly (*Bemisia tabaci*).
**Management**:
- Use resistant varieties like Ty-1, Ty-2.
- Install yellow sticky traps (12/acre) to monitor whiteflies.
- **Chemical Control**: Spray **Imidacloprid 17.8 SL** at 0.5 ml/liter OR **Thiamethoxam 25 WG** at 0.2 g/liter.

### Early Blight
**Symptoms**: Concentric rings (bullseye pattern) on lower leaves.
**Control**:
- Spray **Mancozeb 75 WP** at 2 g/liter.

## Fertilizer Schedule
- **Basal**: Apply 20 tons of FYM/acre.
- **N-P-K**: 50:50:50 kg/acre.
